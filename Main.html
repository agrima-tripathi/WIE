WOMEN IN ENGINEERING

HOME

Despite the fact that it is still a male-dominated business, the Engineering Industry is seeing a growing number of female engineers.

In truth, numerous female engineers have been responsible for some of history's most significant innovations.

They don't always get the credit they deserve, though. Throughout history, engineering has been predominantly a male-dominated field (and it still is in 2021!)

Women have had to overcome huge challenges and break through discriminatory barriers in addition to completing incredible exploits (such as establishing the theory that led to modern-day Wi-Fi).

In this blog we talk about some such women.
